About ttvfav

A simple way to track your favorite Twitch streamers. No account needed, data stored locally.

Quick Tips

  • Click on live channels to open stream
  • Hover over cards to show remove button
  • Status updates every 2 minutes

Privacy

Your data and API credentials are stored locally in cookies. No data is sent to any servers except Twitch API.
